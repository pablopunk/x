## Features

- Private & Free:
  - No account required
  - No data leaves your browser
  - No tracking
  - No ads
  - No cookies
- Draw shapes (rectangles, ellipses, lines, arrows)
- Add text annotations
- Highlight and pixelate areas
- Save or copy to clipboard
- Undo/redo history
- Dark/light theme
- Image history with tabs
